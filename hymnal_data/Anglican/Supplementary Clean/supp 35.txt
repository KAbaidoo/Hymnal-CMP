SUPP. HYMN 35

Full Choir:
Behold, a great prelate, who in his days was pleasing unto God;
Therefore with an oath the Lord hath made him to increase among His people.

Semi-Chorus:
He hath given unto him the blessing of all nations;
And hath confirmed His covenant upon his own head.
