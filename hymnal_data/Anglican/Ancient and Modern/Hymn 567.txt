O my GOD, I fear Thee!
Thou art very high,
Yet to us, Thy children,
Thou art always nigh,
Far removed from mortal sight, Dwelling in eternal light.

O my GOD, I fear Thee!
Yet I come in prayer,
For my SAVIOUR tells me

O my GOD, I fear Thee,
Holy, just, and true;
But, my heavenly FATHER,

Guide me till this life is past, Take me to Thyself at last.
