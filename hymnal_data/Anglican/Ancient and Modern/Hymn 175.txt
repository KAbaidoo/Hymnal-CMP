HYMN 175

Conquering kings their titles take From the foes they captive make:
JESUS, by a nobler deed,
From the thousands He hath freed,
Yes: none other name is given
Unto mortals under heaven,
Which can make the dead arise,
And exalt them to the skies.

That which CHRIST so hardly wrought, that which He so dearly bought,
That salvation, brethren say,
Shall we madly cast away?

Rather gladly for that Name
Bear the cross, endure the shame; Joyfully for Him to die
Is not death but victory,
